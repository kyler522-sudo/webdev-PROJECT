Who Says You Can't? You Do [ REVIEW ]

  • Daniel Chidiac
  • Review

A bibliophile will always find pleasure to write a book review for his/her favorite read. Today I’m going to be writing a review for my most favorite, inspirational and motivational read which is Who Says You Can’t? You Do by Daniel Chidiac. This book clearly explained why dreams are achievable for few people, yet the same dreams are almost impossible to achieve for others.

I coincidentally read a few quotes from this book on Instagram. The quotes were remarkable and unusual, unlike the typical non-practicing-motivational lines. Didn’t believe? Have a look: “It’s evident that we are always obtaining knowledge in this life, but learning requires rapid action. You could watch someone change a car tyre twenty times over, but unless you get down and do it, your knowledge is never tested, nor will you learn.”

Surprised? I too was constantly charmed. It didn’t seem like a common self-improvement guide that I thought would exhaust the living hell out of me. Rather, it sounded genuine. I will concede that I started perusing this book with lifted desires and they were satisfied.

This writing of Daniel Chidiac’s writing has truly touched my mind and soul. It even has the spark to transform thousands of lives. The very first part of this book takes you on a psychological and emotional journey which ends up unlocking the true potential one can possess. If you are really into finding something that has the ability to enrich your mind and soul completely, then believe me your wait is over! Just go and grab this incredible, enlightening, yet extraordinarily challenging book. This writing will make you go deeper in your searches, in your thoughts with many angles before you make a decision to act, as every action has the benefit and/or outcome. One of the best features of this book is that it contains, and offers, all the practical proven things to you! It is highly stimulating because it talks about both failure and success in detail. I found Daniel’s philosophy unique. I strongly recommend that whoever, no matter from which class, can gain much benefit from reading this book. It felt extremely real and not bookish like a lot of books in this genre do.

“Why is it that people usually find true direction in life at a time that seems the most confusing? My opinion is that when all roads seem to be blocked, there is only one way, and that is THROUGH!”

This quote had such a spark that I ended up writing a whole blog just to convince other readers to go and grab this amazing masterpiece. I enjoyed it thoroughly and I found motivation on every page throughout this book that I actually wanted to hear my whole life. It would be a total injustice if I wouldn’t suggest this book to you all. If you’re feeling like an unmotivated soul, or you’re just not happy with your life in this moment, I’d strongly recommend this book to you!
